    Rules

    Turn

    • On your turn, pick a card. One card is a Mystery (first click reveals, second click selects).
    • Penalty Deck: first click reveals, second click confirms and ends your turn.

    Redraw

    • Reveals a penalty card, then after 1s refreshes the 3 cards. Penalty stays open until you close it. Does not change turn.

    Items

    • Shield: blocks your next penalty.
    • Immunity: use on your turn to manually cancel the next drink.
    • Reveal Free: reveals the Mystery card for free.
    • Mirror: Mirror drink to other player.
    • Skip Turn: if used on your turn, you pass immediately; otherwise your next turn is skipped.

    Ditto

    • Sometimes a picked card becomes Ditto. Click again to confirm and apply the stored Ditto effect.
